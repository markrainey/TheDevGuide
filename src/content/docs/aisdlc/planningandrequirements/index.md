The "Blank Page" problem in product development has been solved. In this phase, AI acts as a bridge between high-level business "vibes" and rigorous technical requirements. Using natural language processing, AI tools analyze unstructured data—customer feedback, market trends, and stakeholder interviews—to generate first-draft Product Requirement Documents (PRDs), user stories, and acceptance criteria in seconds.

Readers will learn how to use AI to "stress-test" requirements before a single line of code is written. By simulating thousands of user interactions, AI can identify logical gaps, conflicting requirements, or missing edge cases that typically wouldn't be discovered until the QA phase. This subsection is about mastering the art of Intent Engineering: ensuring that the AI builds the right thing by providing it with a perfectly structured foundation of truth.
