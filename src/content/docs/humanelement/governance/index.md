In an autonomous world, "Governance" is the new "QA." This subsection introduces the technical patterns for Bounded Autonomy. You will learn how to implement Veto Protocols—standardized pause points where an agent must present a "Logic Chain Summary" for human approval before taking a critical action (like a production deployment or a financial transaction).

This content teaches you how to move from direct control to Trust-Based Supervision. We explore "Governance-as-Code," where you define the safety boundaries (guardrails) within which your AI agents are free to operate. For the modern lead engineer, mastering these protocols is the only way to scale your oversight across dozens of parallel agent streams without suffering from "Review Fatigue."
