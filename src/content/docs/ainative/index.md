The **AI-Native Stack** represents the fundamental shift from traditional "human-only" coding to a collaborative ecosystem where artificial intelligence is the primary engine of production. This section strips away the mystery of the modern developer's toolkit, moving beyond simple autocomplete to explore the sophisticated infrastructure of 2026. You will dive into the core "*Foundational Models*" like Gemini 1.5 Pro, GPT-5, and Claude 4, understanding not just how to chat with them, but how to leverage their unique reasoning traces, multimodal inputs, and massive context windows to architect complex systems.

Beyond the models themselves, this section explores the AI-Native IDEs and agentic terminal tools—such as Cursor, Windsurf, and Claude Code—that have replaced the static editors of the past. You should read this content to master the "Orchestration Layer": learning how to connect your local codebase to global intelligence via the Model Context Protocol (MCP) and how to choose between cloud-scale power and the privacy of Local LLMs like Llama 3 running on Ollama. In an era where 70% of code is machine-generated, understanding the "Stack" is no longer about memorizing syntax; it is about mastering the machinery that builds the software for you.
